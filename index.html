Welcome to your journey of embodied healing

Embodied healing with a dollop of sunshine

A gentle invitation to commune with and re-member the wisdom of your body; this wondrous vessel that continues to carry you through the varied seasons. I welcome you to journey alongside me as we attune to the gentle whispers of your nervous system, tend to the hearth of your being and hold space for your expansion. My promise is to offer you companionship for the unfolding, with steadfast assurance and tools for navigating the path. Here, we savour slowness, listen inward, track sensations and allow curiosity to guide us. We seek the sacred in the mundane.

“In the slowness, a shift begins
Feels like grounded presence
We move with the gait of trust
Making space for insight to be felt
Steadier ground beneath the feet
Fuller breaths and deeper sensations
We will neither strive nor fix
With time you may find,
A quiet sense of belonging within the self,
